About

I am a PhD student affiliated with ONERA and IRIT, working on distributed approaches for multimodal route allocation in Mobility-as-a-Service (MaaS) systems. My research focuses on combining optimization, multi-agent systems, and privacy-aware decentralized decision-making.

My work is funded by ONERA and Région Occitanie (MIDOC program) and supervised by Gauthier Picard and Elsy Kaddoum.

Research interests

  • Multi-agent systems
  • Distributed optimization (DCOP, decomposition methods)
  • Mobility as a Service (MaaS)
  • Multimodal transportation systems
  • Multi-criteria optimization

Research work

I proposed the Personalized and Resource-aware Multimodal Routing Problem (PRMRP), a framework for multimodal route planning integrating user preferences, constraints, and resource allocation.

I developed distributed approaches based on:

  • Distributed Constraint Optimization Problems (DCOP)
  • Divide-and-Coordinate (dual decomposition with Lagrangian relaxation)

These methods achieve near-optimal solutions while improving scalability and preserving privacy compared to centralized optimization approaches.
